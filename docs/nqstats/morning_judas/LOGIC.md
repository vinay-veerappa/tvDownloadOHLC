# Morning Judas Logic

**Source**: `JUDAS_INFO.png` (NQStats Website)
**Concept**: Analyzing the "Judas Swing" (False Move) hypothesis between 9:30 AM and 10:00 AM ET.
**Common Belief**: The initial move (9:30-9:40) is often a "fake out" (Judas) that reverses.
**NQStats Claim**: **Myth Busted**. The initial move is usually **real** (Continuation).

## 1. The Definitions
*   **Time A**: 09:30 AM (Open)
*   **Time B**: 09:40 AM (Initial Move Check)
*   **Time C**: 10:00 AM (Resolution)

## 2. The Scenarios & Claims
### Scenario A: "Up Judas" (9:30 -> 9:40 is UP)
*   **Condition**: Price(9:40) > Price(9:30).
*   **Outcome 1 (Reversal/False Swing)**: Price(10:00) < Price(9:30). (Did it fully reverse the gains?) -> **Claim: 36%**.
*   **Outcome 2 (Continuation)**: Price(10:00) > Price(9:40). (Did it continue going up?) -> **Claim: 64%**.

### Scenario B: "Down Judas" (9:30 -> 9:40 is DOWN)
*   **Condition**: Price(9:40) < Price(9:30).
*   **Outcome 1 (Reversal)**: Price(10:00) > Price(9:30). -> **Claim: 30%**.
*   **Outcome 2 (Continuation)**: Price(10:00) < Price(9:40). -> **Claim: 70%**.

## 3. Operational Strategy
*   **Bias**: If 9:30-9:40 is strong, **Follow the trend**. Do not fade it expecting a "Judas" reversal. The reversal is the lower probability event (~30-36%).
