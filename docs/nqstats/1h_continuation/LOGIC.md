# 1H Continuation Logic

**Source**: `1H_INFO.png` (NQStats Website)
**Concept**: Using the **09:00-10:00 AM ET Candle** color to predict the direction of the rest of the session.

## 1. The Key Metric (9 AM Hour)
*   **The Hour**: 09:00 AM to 10:00 AM ET. (Includes the 09:30 Equity Open).
*   **Condition**:
    *   **Green Hour**: 10:00 Close > 09:00 Open.
    *   **Red Hour**: 10:00 Close < 09:00 Open.

## 2. The Statistical Claims (NQ)
*   **Scenario A: 9 AM Hour is GREEN**:
    *   **NY Session Continuation**: Probability that NY Session (09:30 Open to 16:00 Close) is also GREEN (Close > Open) is **~70%**.
    *   **Full Session Continuation**: Probability that Daily Candle is Green is **67%**.

*   **Scenario B: 9 AM Hour is RED**:
    *   **NY Session Continuation**: Probability that NY Session is RED is **60%**.

## 3. The 6 PM Metric (Overnight)
*   Also mentions the 6 PM (18:00) opening hour.
*   Claim: 6 PM Green -> Full Session Green 59% (Weak Edge).

## 4. Operational Strategy
*   Wait for **10:00 AM** ET.
*   Check the 1H Candle (09:00-10:00).
*   **Bias**: If Green, look for Longs / Expect NY Close > NY Open.
*   **Bias**: If Red, look for Shorts (weaker edge).
