## 15. Risk Mitigation

### Risks from Adding AI:
- **Cost:** LLM API costs can be high
  - *Mitigation:* Message limits, caching, local models option
  
- **Complexity:** AI features add development complexity
  - *Mitigation:* Phase AI in two stages, MVP first
  
- **Quality:** AI responses may not always be accurate
  - *Mitigation:* Clear disclaimers, confidence indicators, human oversight
  
- **Privacy:** Users concerned about data sharing
  - *Mitigation:* Explicit opt-in, local AI option, transparency

### Competitive Risks:
- **TradesViz adds our features:** VVIX, economic calendar
  - *Mitigation:* First-mover advantage, better integration, futures focus
  
- **New entrant:** Another AI-powered journal
  - *Mitigation:* Unique combination of features, self-hosting option
  
- **Market size:** Futures traders may be too niche
  - *Mitigation:* Excellent tool for niche is better than mediocre tool for everyone
